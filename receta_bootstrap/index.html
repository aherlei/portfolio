Tony's Healthy recipe

Ingredients

  • - 1 liter of carbonated water (sparkling water)
  • - 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
  • - 1/4 cup fresh orange juice
  • - 1/2 cup fresh pineapple juice
  • - 1/4 cup maple syrup or honey (adjust according to your sweetness preference)
  • - A pinch of salt

Instructions

  • 1. In a large container, mix the carbonated water with the lemon, orange, and pineapple juices.
  • 2. Add the maple syrup or honey and a pinch of salt.
  • 3. Stir the mixture well until the syrup is completely dissolved and all the ingredients are well combined.
  • 4. Taste the mixture and adjust the sweetness level if necessary by adding more maple syrup or honey according to your taste.
  • 5. Refrigerate the healthy "Coca-Cola" for at least 1 hour before serving, as this will allow the flavors to blend and chill properly.
  • 6. Serve over ice and enjoy this healthier version of a classic beverage.
