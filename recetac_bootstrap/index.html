Tony's Homemade Recipe

Ingredients

  • - 1 liter of carbonated water (sparkling water)
  • - 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
  • - 1/4 cup fresh orange juice
  • - 1/2 cup fresh pineapple juice
  • - 1/4 cup maple syrup or honey (adjust according to your sweetness preference)
  • - A pinch of salt

Instructions

  • In a large container, mix the carbonated water with the lemon, orange, and pineapple juices.
  • Add the maple syrup or honey and a pinch of salt.
  • Stir the mixture well until the syrup is completely dissolved and all the ingredients are well combined.
  • Taste the mixture and adjust the sweetness level if necessary by adding more maple syrup or honey according to your taste.
  • Refrigerate the healthy "Coca-Cola" for at least 1 hour before serving, as this will allow the flavors to blend and chill properly.
  • Serve over ice and enjoy this healthier version of a classic beverage.
